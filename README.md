# Analog & Mixed-Signal Design Portfolio

Welcome! This repository serves as a technical portfolio showcasing my independent and academic design work in analog circuit modeling, LTspice transient/AC simulations, and hardware prototyping. 

## Portfolio Projects

### 1. [Multi-Stage Analog Audio Effects Pedal (Voice Distorter)](./Voice-Distorter-Pedal/)
An analog signal conditioning circuit optimized for processing human voice frequencies ($250\text{ Hz}$ to $4\text{ kHz}$).
* **Core Topologies:** Active op-amp gain stages, nonlinear diode clipping networks, and active bandpass filtering.
* **Key Achievements:** Modeled and tuned the circuit's frequency-selective stages in LTspice to sweep dynamic range parameters before physical breadboard deployment.

### 2. [AC Adapter Modeling & Power Supply Simulation](./AC-Adapter-Power-Supply/)
A complete system-level model of a high-current AC-to-DC power supply designed for stable laptop power delivery.
* **Core Topologies:** Transformer step-down modeling, full-wave rectification, and multi-stage Pi ($\pi$) filter networks.
* **Key Achievements:** Formally derived theoretical ripple voltage metrics and verified transient performance under heavy variable resistive loads ($2\,\Omega$ to $6\,\Omega$).
